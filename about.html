Om Tomas

My name is Tomas Nordlander. I am a freelance photographer with a focus on mostly outdoor, events and sport. Photography has always been a passion of mine but I started pursuing it more rigorously 10 years ago. With time I have upgraded both my equipment and photography skills. My most recent award is winning the newspaper NWT's winter picture competition 2020-2021. Based in western Sweden I can work with both local and international clients.

Want to know more? Skicka ett medelande till mig.

What kind of gear do I use?

My current camera is Nikon D850. The lenses I mostly combine with it are Nikon 85mm 1:1.8, Sigma 14-24mm 1:2.8, Sigma 70-200mm 1:2.8, Sigma 50mm 1:1.4 and Tamron 24-70mm 1:2.8. For flash I use Nikon Speedlight SB-700. I am also proficient in editing my photos using Adobe Photoshop.

Can I use pictures from your website?

As a general rule, if you use my content for commercial purposes or in a commercial setting, then the answer is "Not without my permission". Such as if you have advertisements anywhere on your site or if it has any financial or money making aspect to it. All my content is copyright Tomas Nordlander. If you are going to use my content, I would appreciate it if you gave credit on the images with links back to their source. Thank you.

If you have any questions regarding this please send me a message here.
